Key focus areas for success in 2026

Veterinary professionals from our Customer Advisory Board shared with us their goals for 2026 and strategies for meeting them. Here's what topped the list:

Streamline the client experience

Leverage online booking and provide clear pre-visit guidance for smooth, efficient visits.
 

Boost revenue and profitability

Focus on preventive care, wellness plans, and being strategic when selecting solutions.
 

Drive operational efficiency

Embrace technology and increase staff training on digital tools to free up time.
 

Grow community reputation

Engage with the local community and share expertise to bring more patients through the door.

Reach out to your account manager to review your practice's goals and strategize for success.

Product update

Royal Canin SKU change

Starting January 2026, Royal Canin is discontinuing Canine Glycobalance Dry Dog Food, 7.7 lb (SKU 40488677EA) and updating the formulas for their other canine Glycobalance dry and wet food products (SKUs 40427414EA, 40488617EA, and 40038324EA).

 

Clients with existing AutoShip orders for discontinued products will receive an email from Vetsource letting them know about the discontinued product and encouraging them to connect with your practice to consult with you to update their orders.

Please note this does not apply to feline Glycobalance food products.
